Tanghulu

Description

Have you ever wondered how deliciously crunchy a cockroach might be but was never ever able to fullfill that dream due to unrelenting societal standards?

Well fear not today for this recipe will help you concoct the most satisfying and crispy sugar-coated candy with the same level, if not more, of crunchiness as a wild cockroach hiding under your home's refrigerator.

It's very simple to make that even cockroaches could probably be able to if only they were blessed with limbs like us superior human mammals.

Well what are we waiting for? Let's jump right in!

Ingredients

  • Any fruit of your liking
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Skewers

Steps

      Prepare the Fruit
    1. Wash the fruit and completely dry them, otherwise the sugar won't stick.
    2. Remove any undesirable parts of the fruit diminishing the texture and taste of the fruit, ie. leaves, stems, seeds.
    3. If necessary, cut your fruit into bite-size pieces.
    4. Poke your fruit pieces onto the skewers. For best results, limit to 3 pieces per skewer.
